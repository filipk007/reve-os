# Approval Process — {{client_name}}

## Content Approval Workflow
1. **Draft submitted** — delivered via portal update (type: deliverable)
2. **Client reviews** — 48h review window
3. **Feedback round** — up to 2 revision rounds included
4. **Final approval** — client confirms via portal or Slack
5. **Goes live** — deployed within 24h of approval

## Turnaround Times
| Stage | SLA |
|-------|-----|
| Initial draft | 3 business days |
| Revision round | 2 business days |
| Final deployment | 1 business day |

## Revision Policy
- 2 rounds of revisions included per deliverable
- Additional rounds billed at hourly rate
- "Revision" = changes to existing content, not new scope

## Auto-Approval
If no response within 5 business days of submission, content is considered approved and will go live.

## Approval Authority
- **Day-to-day content:** Primary contact
- **Brand/messaging changes:** Marketing lead
- **Budget/scope changes:** Executive sponsor
