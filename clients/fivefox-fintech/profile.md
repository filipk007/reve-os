# FiveFox Media — Fintech Performance Marketing

## Who They Are
Small agency specialized in performance marketing for fintech lenders. UK/EU-rooted,
expanding across Baltics, Nordics, DACH, Benelux, and selectively into US/AUS/CAN.
Not a generalist marketing partner — a diagnostic specialist that fixes the tracking
and creative infrastructure between a paid click and a funded loan. Peer-to-peer
posture with growth/marketing leads, understated not hype-driven.

## What They Sell
Performance marketing services for licensed digital lenders — paid media (Meta,
Google, TikTok), funnel optimization, tracking infrastructure (CAPI, backend event
schemas), compliance-ready creative, and conversion rate improvement across digital
lending funnels. Outcome they're paid for: funded loans, not applications.

## Value Proposition
- Optimize for funded loans, not applications — the metric that actually matters to lenders
- Fix tracking infrastructure (CAPI, backend events, GTM) so the algorithm optimizes for the right thing
- Compliance-ready creative that gets approved first time — no wasted cycles on rejected ads
- Proprietary consent retargeting mechanism that unlocks GDPR-compliant retargeting on Meta for financial products

## Tone Preferences
- **Voice:** Peer-to-peer with growth/marketing leads, diagnostic not pitching
- **Formality:** Professional but direct — UK/EU sensibility, understated not hype-driven
- **Region:** UK/EU primary, secondary US/AUS/CAN
- **Sentence length:** Short punchy. 75-125 words per email (under 100 for follow-ups)
- **Forbidden phrases:** "leverage", "synergy", "optimize your go-to-market motion", compliment openers ("I love what you're doing"), #1 claims, guaranteed results, template-sounding lines, em dashes
- **Required:** A specific number (3.4x, 42%, 2.7x), low-friction CTA (never ask for time), something ICP-specific that only makes sense to a lending team
- **Subject lines:** Under 40 chars, lowercase, no punctuation tricks
