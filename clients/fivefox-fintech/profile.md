# FiveFox Media — Fintech Performance Marketing

## Company
- **Domain:** fivefox.media
- **Industry:** Performance marketing agency (fintech lending vertical)
- **Size:** Small agency
- **Stage:** Established, expanding into new verticals

## What They Sell
Performance marketing services for fintech lenders — paid media (Meta, Google, TikTok), funnel optimization, tracking infrastructure, compliance-ready creative, and conversion rate improvement across digital lending funnels. They drive funded loans, not vanity metrics.

## Target ICP
- **Firmographics:** Licensed digital lenders, 20-500 employees (sweet spot 50-200), Series A+ or profitable self-funded, UK/EU primary (Baltics, Nordics, DACH, Benelux), secondary US/AUS/CAN
- **Titles:** Head of Growth, Head of Marketing, CMO, Head of Acquisition, Growth Lead, Performance Marketing Manager, Digital Marketing Director
- **Secondary:** CEO/COO (smaller lenders), Head of Product (funnel UX), CFO (unit economics)
- **Verticals:** Personal loans, auto finance, BNPL, green energy loans, medical credit, working capital, merchant cash advances, earned wage access

## Competitive Landscape
- **CSTMR** (US): Big brand credibility (LendingTree, Credit Karma), broad services, measures SEO/traffic not funded loans
- **Growth Gorilla** (UK): Generalist fintech, strong on influencer/UGC, no lending-specific funded loan metrics
- **Bandera** (Emerging markets): Born from in-house lending team, broad services, price/convenience play

**What nobody says (our gap):**
- Nobody talks about lending funnel as infrastructure (CAPI, backend events, conversion schema)
- Nobody measures in funded volume — everyone reports leads/sign-ups
- Nobody positions as diagnostic specialist ("we find what's broken")
- Nobody has a consent retargeting mechanism for Special Ads Category

## Value Proposition
1. Optimize for funded loans, not applications — the metric that actually matters to lenders
2. Fix tracking infrastructure (CAPI, backend events, GTM) so the algorithm optimizes for the right thing
3. Compliance-ready creative that gets approved first time — no wasted cycles on rejected ads
4. Proprietary consent retargeting mechanism that unlocks GDPR-compliant retargeting on Meta for financial products

## Tone Preferences
- **Formality:** Professional but direct — UK/EU sensibility, understated not hype-driven
- **Approach:** Peer-to-peer with growth/marketing leads, data-informed but not jargon-heavy
- **Email length:** 75-125 words (under 100 for follow-ups)
- **Things to avoid:** "Leverage", "synergy", "optimize your go-to-market motion", compliment openers ("I love what you're doing"), claims about being #1, guaranteed results, anything that sounds like a template
- **Always include:** A specific number/result (3.4x, 42%, 2.7x), low-friction CTA (never ask for their time), something ICP-specific that only makes sense to a lending team
- **Subject lines:** Under 40 chars, lowercase, no punctuation tricks

## Campaign Angles

### Angle A — "The Funded Loan Gap"
- **USE WHEN:** Company IS running paid ads but results are mediocre, or can't see what's driving funded loans
- **SIGNALS:** Active ad accounts, high spend, mediocre results, no backend tracking, no CAPI, recently parted with agency
- **HOOK:** "Every agency optimizes for applications. We optimize for funded loans. Most lenders are leaking volume between the click and the funded loan — broken tracking, wrong conversion events, no visibility past the form fill."
- **PROOF:** Paynt: CAPI + event schema aligned to approved completions → 3.4x application completions, 42% CAC drop. Inbank: rebuilt tracking → 2.7x loan volume in 3 months.

### Angle B — "The Compliance-Creative Engine"
- **USE WHEN:** Company is NOT running ads yet, or just started, hiring first growth role
- **SIGNALS:** No active paid media, recent funding, new to market, launching first campaigns
- **HOOK:** "We write ads that get approved the first time — because we know what regulators flag before they do. No rejected creatives, no wasted cycles."
- **PROOF:** Paynt — trust-first creative matching compliance language, 3.4x completions.

### Angle C — "The Consent Retargeting Mechanism"
- **USE WHEN:** EU-based lender running or planning Meta/TikTok ads, blocked by Special Ads Category + GDPR
- **SIGNALS:** EU headquarters, GDPR concerns, Special Ads Category kills targeting, broad campaigns with no retargeting
- **HOOK:** "There's a GDPR-compliant way to retarget lending products on Meta that almost nobody in Europe uses."
- **MECHANISM:** Broad traffic → two-step consent on-site (financial marketing specific) → tracking ID loads → Meta Pixel picks up → compliant retargetable custom audience
- **PROOF:** Inbank — 2.7x growth, 19% new clients from remarketing, <1 EUR per form fill.
