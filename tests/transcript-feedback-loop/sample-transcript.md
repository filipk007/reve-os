# Discovery Call - Fivefox Media + Sarah Chen, Head of Growth at LendCo

**Date:** 2026-04-05
**Attendees:** Filip (Fivefox), Sarah Chen (LendCo - Head of Growth)
**Duration:** 32 minutes

---

**Filip:** Sarah, thanks for jumping on. To kick off, what's the biggest thing on your plate right now around acquisition?

**Sarah:** Honestly? We just closed Series B in February and the board's expecting us to triple funded volume by end of year. I know that sounds aggressive but that's the mandate. The problem is I have no visibility into what's actually happening between someone clicking an ad and a loan getting funded. Our CAC on applications looks fine but the funded loan CAC is brutal and I can't explain why.

**Filip:** That's actually one of the most common things we hear from lending teams. The application-to-funded gap is where everything breaks. What's your current tracking setup look like?

**Sarah:** We're on Meta and Google primarily, GA4 with some custom events but honestly nobody on my team really trusts the data. We've been burned. Last agency we worked with kept telling us our CAC was great and then we'd look at funded volume and it didn't add up.

**Filip:** When did you part ways with them?

**Sarah:** Two months ago. It was painful. They were optimizing for form-fills and reporting that as success.

**Filip:** Yeah - so to be honest, I hate the phrase "data-driven marketing." Everyone says it and nobody knows what it means. What we actually do is rebuild the tracking infrastructure first - CAPI, backend conversion events tied to approved loans, the whole pipe - before we touch a single ad. Most lenders we work with see funded volume go up 2-3x in the first quarter just from that.

**Sarah:** Okay that's interesting. How long does that usually take?

**Filip:** First pipe rebuilt in 2-3 weeks usually. We don't promise overnight. And to be transparent, we ask for read access to your ad accounts and your loan management system on day one. If you're not comfortable with that, we're not the right fit.

**Sarah:** That's actually refreshing. The last agency wanted us to give them everything but never told us what they were doing with it. One thing though - I've heard from other lenders that GDPR makes retargeting basically impossible on Meta in Europe right now. We're UK so we're feeling that. Is that something you've solved?

**Filip:** Yes - that's actually one of the things we built specifically for this. There's a consent-based retargeting mechanism we developed. Happy to walk you through it if it's relevant.

**Sarah:** Definitely. Send me a one-pager on it.

**Filip:** Sure. One last thing - what's the worst part of how outbound agencies usually pitch you?

**Sarah:** Honestly the "synergy" emails. And anything that says "let's hop on a quick 30 minute call." If someone says quick I know it won't be quick. Also when they pretend to know about my company and clearly used ChatGPT - I can spot it in two seconds. The one thing that actually works on me is when someone shows me they actually understand the lending funnel - someone said "the gap between submitted and funded is where ad spend dies" and I replied within an hour.

**Filip:** Noted. Anything else you'd want me to know?

**Sarah:** We're hiring a Performance Marketing Manager right now. So if this works out we'd be running it together with whoever joins.

**Filip:** Got it. Thanks Sarah.
