Summary
CRM Magnetics presented their 4-layer optimization framework and flexible service models to Emblea for resolving their manual HubSpot lead management and automation issues.

Emblea Business Development Challenges
Emily Martha Geras shared that Emblea focuses outbound business development on HR and benefit leaders and faces significant difficulties with manual lead importation and sequence automation in HubSpot. Leads are manually added to sequences and manually moved through stages, creating risks of duplicate outreach and poor tracking.

CRM Optimization Framework Proposed
Landon Schlag introduced a 4-layer framework, starting with the Foundation layer to clean data and define lifecycle stages for proper lead movement, addressing concerns about merging duplicates. The Automation layer focuses on eliminating manual work via automated lead routing and consistent follow-up sequences, including integrating tools like ZoomInfo.

Service Model and Project Scope
CRM Magnetics offers flexible engagement, including hourly work or a retainer model with Silver, Gold, or Platinum tiers, with Gold being the most common option at 20 hours per month. The discussion highlighted the need for improved Visibility via dashboards to track BD activity and sales pipeline movement, which is currently lacking.
