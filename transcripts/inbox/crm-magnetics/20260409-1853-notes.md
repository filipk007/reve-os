Summary
Initial strategy and scoping session discussed solutions for HubSpot client management, contract automation, and lead pipeline optimization.

Confidential Client Visibility Solution
The true way to hide confidential contacts is using HubSpot permissions and team structures; Elizabeth Boocks agreed to provide a list of users and contacts to enable team and automation building.

Automation for Contracts and Renewals
An automated workflow was proposed to generate renewal deals for won deals 3 or 6 months before contract expiration for tracking renewal rates. A demonstration of a highly automated contract generation and signature system via Portant was also provided.

Optimizing Lead and Deal Pipelines
It was decided that the team is creating deals too early in the process, resulting in an overwhelming number of intake deals, and they should instead manage less qualified contacts at the MQL and SQL stage. The best solution for managing deals created by no-shows is to use a deal property like 'demo status' tied to automation, rather than separate deal stages.
